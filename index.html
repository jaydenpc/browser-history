Introduction

Web browsers have come a long way since the early days of the internet. From the first graphical browser, Mosaic, to the modern-day giants like Chrome and Firefox, the history of browsers is a fascinating journey through technological innovation and competition. In this article, we will explore the evolution of web browsers, highlighting key milestones and the impact they have had on how we access and interact with the web.
