A Brief history of IE

Internet Explorer (IE) was developed by Microsoft and released in 1995. It quickly became the dominant web browser, reaching a peak market share of over 90% inthe early 2000s. However, IE's dominance was challenged by the rise of alternative browsers like Firefox and Chrome, which offered better performance, security, and support for modern web standards. Microsoft eventually discontinued support for Internet Explorer in 2022, marking the end of an era for one of the most iconic web browsers in history.
